Anti-Wrinkle Injections

Soften fine lines while preserving the expression that makes your face yours. Our anti-wrinkle approach is conservative — we relax muscle activity, we don't freeze it.

What it treats

  • Forehead lines (horizontal)
  • Frown lines (between the brows / "11 lines")
  • Crow's feet (around the eyes)
  • Bunny lines (across the bridge of the nose)
  • Lip flip and gummy smile (advanced)
  • Jawline slimming (masseter)

Frequently asked

Will I look frozen or expressionless?

No. We deliberately use conservative dosing so your expression is softened, not lost. Many clients say no one can tell they have had anything done — they just look more rested.

How soon will I see results?

You'll begin to notice the effect at day 3, with the full result visible at two weeks. We always book a complimentary review at this point to fine-tune if needed.

Are there side effects?

Mild bruising or pinpoint redness at the injection site is possible, usually fading within 24 hours. Serious side effects are very rare in trained, experienced hands.

Can I exercise afterwards?

We ask you to avoid intense exercise, lying flat, hot saunas and alcohol for the rest of the day. Normal activity from the next morning is fine.
