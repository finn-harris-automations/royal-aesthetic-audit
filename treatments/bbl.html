Refined, restrained body contouring using high-grade hyaluronic-acid filler — no surgery, no fat transfer, no general anaesthetic. A natural-looking lift designed around your existing shape.

  • Loss of upper-glute volume from age or weight loss
  • "Hip dips" or asymmetry between sides

  • Subtle projection and roundness without surgery

Frequently asked

How is this different from a surgical BBL?

Surgical BBL involves transferring fat under general anaesthetic — significant cost, recovery and risk. Our HA-based approach is far more conservative: non-surgical, walk-in walk-out, designed for refinement rather than dramatic transformation.

Will it look natural?

Roxana works conservatively, often building over more than one session. We will never inject more than your anatomy can comfortably accommodate. You should look like a more refined version of your shape — not someone else.

How long does it last?

Results typically last 12–18 months. Top-ups can be smaller and less expensive than the initial session, as the area retains some volume each time.

Is there downtime?

Mild swelling and possible bruising for 1–2 weeks. We ask you to avoid prolonged sitting, intense exercise, hot saunas and tight-fitting clothing for the first 7 days. Most clients return to gentle work the next day.

Can it be reversed?

Yes — HA filler can be safely dissolved with hyaluronidase. This is one of the key safety advantages over fat transfer or permanent fillers.
