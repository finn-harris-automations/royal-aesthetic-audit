Lip Filler

Beautifully balanced, natural-looking lips. Hyaluronic-acid filler delivered with precision — designed to complement your face, not overpower it.

What it treats

  • Loss of definition or shape
  • Asymmetry between upper and lower lip
  • Vertical lip lines and "smoker's lines"
  • Loss of volume from age or genetics
  • Re-shaping the cupid's bow

Frequently asked

Will my lips look "done"?

Not under our care. Roxana works conservatively, building gradually if needed. We'd rather you came back for more than over-fill once.

Is it painful?

Most clients describe it as a small pinch and pressure rather than pain. Numbing cream is applied before treatment, and the filler itself contains lidocaine.

Can it be reversed?

Yes. Hyaluronic-acid filler can be safely dissolved with hyaluronidase if you ever change your mind.

How long until I look "normal"?

Initial swelling settles in 24–48 hours. The final shape is visible at around 2 weeks once the filler has integrated.
