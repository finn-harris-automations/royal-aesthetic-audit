Bio-Remodelling

Profhilo

The original "injectable moisturiser." Profhilo is a uniquely stable hyaluronic-acid product that doesn't add volume — it bio-stimulates the skin from within, restoring firmness, hydration and luminosity.

What it treats

  • Skin laxity and loss of bounce
  • Crepey or dehydrated-looking skin
  • Dullness and loss of glow
  • Fine surface lines (especially across the cheeks and neck)
  • Loss of elasticity from age, sun damage or weight loss

Treatment details

  • Appointment time: 30 minutes

  • Best results from a course of two, four weeks apart
  • Effects visible from week 4; lasting around 6 months per course

Frequently asked

Will it change the shape of my face?

No. Unlike traditional filler, Profhilo spreads under the skin to bio-stimulate collagen and elastin. It firms and hydrates without altering volume or shape.

How quickly will I see results?

Subtle hydration improvements appear within a week. The full firming and luminosity effect develops over 4–8 weeks, especially after the second treatment.

Is there downtime?

Five small bumps appear at the injection points and settle within 12–24 hours. Most clients return to normal life immediately, with light makeup possible the next day.

Why a course of two?

Profhilo is engineered to release in two phases. The second treatment four weeks later compounds the bio-stimulation effect — a single session does not deliver the full result.
