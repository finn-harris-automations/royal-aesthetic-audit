Skin Quality

Skin Boosters

Hydration, from within. Skin boosters are micro-injections of pure, lightly cross-linked hyaluronic acid — delivering deep moisture, improved texture and a glass-like quality you simply cannot get from skincare alone.

What it treats

  • Dry, dehydrated skin
  • Loss of glow and luminosity
  • Uneven texture, enlarged pores
  • Fine surface lines (smoker's lines, perioral lines)
  • Crepey skin under the eyes, on the neck and décolleté

Frequently asked

How is this different from Profhilo?

Profhilo bio-stimulates collagen and firms the deeper structure. Skin boosters work nearer the surface, focused on hydration, glow and texture. They are often used together — different targets, complementary results.

Will I see immediate results?

Some hydration improvement is visible within days, but the meaningful glow and texture change builds over 2–4 weeks, especially after a second session.

Can I have skin boosters under my eyes?

Yes — we use specific products formulated for the under-eye area. It's one of our most-requested treatments for tired-looking eyes, and pairs well with Profhilo or anti-wrinkle treatment.

What's the downtime?

Tiny injection bumps appear immediately and usually settle within 12–24 hours. Light makeup the next day is fine. Most clients book treatments mid-week and feel themselves by the weekend.
