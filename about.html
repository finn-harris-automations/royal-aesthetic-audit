Meet Roxana

Boutique by design. Owner-operated by choice. Roxana is the only pair of hands you'll ever feel at Royal Aesthetic London.

Her Story

A practitioner first.
An owner second.

Roxana founded Royal Aesthetic London with a quiet conviction: that aesthetics, done well, is an act of restraint. The best treatments are the ones nobody can name — only that you look rested, more like yourself, somehow lighter.

After years working across busier London clinics, Roxana opened her own space to do things differently. No rotating practitioners. No commission targets. No hard sells. Just a small handful of treatments she has perfected — delivered to the standard she would expect for herself.

It is why some clients have stayed with her since 2017. And why almost every new client comes through a quiet recommendation from one of them.

Philosophy

Three principles that shape every appointment

01

Less, but better

The temptation in aesthetics is to do more. We do less — and only what genuinely serves the face. Sometimes the most considered advice is to wait.

02

Anatomy over trend

Trends move. Faces don't. Treatments are designed around your unique facial anatomy and proportions, not whatever is fashionable on social media.

03

Your face, your timeline

Aesthetics is a long conversation, not a single appointment. We'll plan in seasons and years — refining gently as life and your face change.

In Roxana's Words
I do not believe in the production line approach to aesthetics. Every face is different, every life is different, and every treatment plan should reflect that. My clients trust me with their face — that is a relationship, not a transaction.
— Roxana, Founder
