Frequent Questions

Before your first visit

Is the consultation really complimentary?

Yes. Your first 30-minute consultation with Roxana is complimentary, with no obligation to proceed. We will discuss your goals, assess your face, and walk you through realistic options — that is all. If you are not ready, you are not ready.

Do you take walk-ins?

No. The clinic operates strictly by appointment so that every client has Roxana's full attention, in private, without the rush or noise of a busier salon.

How do I prepare for my first appointment?

Avoid alcohol and blood-thinning supplements (such as fish oil, vitamin E and ibuprofen) for 48 hours beforehand if possible. Otherwise, simply arrive with clean skin and any photos that have inspired you. Detailed pre-treatment guidance will be sent on confirmation.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for 48 hours' notice for any cancellation or rescheduling. Late cancellations may forfeit a portion of any deposit paid — full details are sent on booking.

Are payment plans available?

Yes — for treatment courses (such as Profhilo or skin booster packages) we offer split-payment plans. Speak to Roxana on consultation.
