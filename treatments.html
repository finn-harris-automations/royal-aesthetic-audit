What to Expect

Every visit follows the same considered rhythm

01

Consultation

We begin with a private, unhurried conversation about your goals, your face, and your concerns. No pressure to commit on the day.

02

Treatment

All treatments are delivered personally by Roxana, in a calm, immaculately clean clinic environment. Comfort is non-negotiable.

03

Aftercare

You'll leave with full aftercare guidance and direct contact for any questions. A two-week follow-up is included on every appointment.
